## Expanded Details
### Nature - Idealist
Regain Willpower when your beliefs are tested to the breaking point yet do not
fail.

### Backgrounds
#### Arcane V
Add Arcane dots as dice on all Stealth rolls; opponents reduce their Perception
or Investigation dice pools by Arcane dots to track you down.
#### Avatar V
You can absorb or expend five points of Quintessence.
#### Chantry I
You have membership in the Normal Chantry, though most of the perks of your
membership are through your minder, Ilyn Veshann bani Quesitor.
#### Sanctum II
You have consecrated your and Ilyn's living room to Magick. Magic performed in
this space by you and your allies is always coincidental, and rituals have their
difficulty reduced by 1. Furthermore, the space has an Arcane rating equal to
its Sanctum rating, and the Gauntlet is reduced by one within it. Finally, it
has a stock of materials and supplies you can use for your works.

### Merits
#### Lightning Calculator I
The difficulty of all math- and calculation-based rolls is reduced by -2. Simple
calculations take no time at all, while sophisticated problems may demand a turn
or so.

### Flaws
#### Permanent Paradox Flaw VI
Reality is actively trying to erase you. People forget you, documents get lost,
and files get corrupted. Your existence is fleeting and ephemeral, and if
Paradox had its way, would be measured in seconds.
#### Probationary Member IV
As a Tremere from before they ~~were cool~~ went evil, *and* someone from a
long-bygone era without anyone to vouch for you in the modern day, you're very
much on probation with the current administration of the Order of Hermes. They
want you as a member, don't get them wrong, but they want you where they can see
you and have little patience for nonsense.
#### Rival House I
There are those around who take issue with a modern-day Tremere mage walking
around their chantry, and aren't above petty hostility when they can get away
with it.
#### Anachronism I
You are a little weird and out-of-touch. Add +1 to the difficulty of
understanding current technology and modes of behavior.
